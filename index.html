Welcome to the Treasure TROVE.

A Tool for Rapid Object Vetting and Examination


TROVE is a new multi-messenger collaboration comprised primarily of researchers at the University of Arizona, UC San Diego and Northwestern University. The goal of this project is to develop a public, open-source, API-enabled platform and website for the real time evaluation of transient objects associated with multimessenger astrophysics events. We are actively planning and building this new piece of time domain infrastructure for broad usage in the community. TROVE is under active development and is currently in alpha.
